Designer

Company: Game Mod Studios

Location: Remote

Job Description:

Game Mod Studios is seeking a talented Designer to join our team. As a Designer, you will play a crucial role in creating visually stunning and immersive experiences for our games and mods. If you have a passion for game design and a strong creative vision, we want to hear from you!

Responsibilities:

  1. Create and design engaging game assets, including characters, environments, and UI elements.
  2. Collaborate with developers and other designers to ensure a seamless integration of design elements.

Requirements:

  1. Proven experience in game design with a strong portfolio showcasing your work.
  2. Proficiency in design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.).
