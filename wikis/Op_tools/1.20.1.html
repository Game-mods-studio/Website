Op_tools Mod Wiki - Version 1.20.1

Overview

Op_tools is a Minecraft mod developed by Game Mod Studios, designed to enhance the player's experience by introducing a powerful array of tools and armor that elevate them to near-godlike status within the game. The mod enriches gameplay through the addition of new ores, blocks, armor, and tools.

Installation

To access the mod, players must install FabricApi, specifically for the fabric version of the mod. Ensure compatibility with Minecraft version 1.20.1.

Ores and Ingots

The centerpiece of Op_tools is the op_ore, found below level 0. Players can smelt op_ore into op_ingots, which serve as the foundation for crafting op tools and armor.

Crafting

Op_tools introduces a variety of crafting options using op_ingots. Refer to the included guide book for detailed recipes on crafting op_swords, op_pickaxes, op_armor, and more.

Features

Op_sword

Attack Damage: Boasting an impressive 1002 attack damage, the op_sword is a formidable weapon in the player's arsenal.

Op_armor

Benefits: Provides substantial benefits, including 5 knockback resistance, enhancing the player's survivability.

Durability

Exceptional Durability: All tools within the mod exhibit exceptional durability with a staggering 10,000 uses.

Fire Resistance

Environmental Protection: Op_tools are fire-resistant, providing an additional layer of protection against environmental hazards.

Conclusion

Op_tools fundamentally transforms the Minecraft experience by providing players with potent tools and armor. The mod fosters a sense of mastery and empowerment within the game, allowing players to explore and conquer the Minecraft universe with unparalleled strength.
